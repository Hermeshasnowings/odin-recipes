Egg Fried Rice

Description

A quick recipe for takeaway-style egg fried rice with loads of flavour! Check out THE MOST IMPORTANT TIP for ensuring your rice isn't sticky or claggy. Such a great dish to serve up as part of a homemade Chinese fakeaway!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion - peeled and diced
  • 6 cloves garlic - peeled and chopped/crushed
  • 2 cloves garlic - peeled and minced
  • 800 g (4 cups) boiled and cooled long-grain rice - (this is about 1 â…“ cups (300g) dried rice that has been boiled in water

Steps

  1. Fry onion in a little oil in the wok, then add minced garlic and cook for further 30 seconds.
  2. Add the rice, knock up the heat to high and drizzle over a little sesame oil. Toss everything together with a spatula. Note: Using a spatula is important as it allows you to get underneath the rice and toss it, rather than break up the rice grains (if you were to stir with a spoon for example).
  3. Once the rice is hot, push the rice over to the side of the pan and crack in two eggs
